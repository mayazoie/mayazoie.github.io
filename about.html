About

I love videography, photography, graphic design, and digital art. I have a wide range of personal and commission work.

I have always loved art in all of its forms. I am mainly self-taught, and the majority of my work is self-initiated.

I am proficient in Final Cut Pro X, Adobe After Effects, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premier, Canva, Procreate, and more! I am skilled in R and am learning HTML and CSS.

I typically rely on my Canon Powershot G7 X Mark III or my Nikon D5100. Oftentimes, my iPhone 13 Pro Max is more accessible, though. And I love to mess around with my DJI Mavic Air 2! Drones fascinate me!

Living in Florida for nearly 21 years, I've enjoyed perfect weather and the ocean's magnificence year-round. Thus, I was inspired to study the climate in addition to journalism. I hope to one day combine my passions and work as a marine videographer and photographer.
