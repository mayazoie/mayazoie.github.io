GottCustoms

Custom Merchandise

One day, while sick in bed, I was doodling on my iPad when my boyfriend asked me to design a piece of clothing for him to wear for his college's rivalry basketball game. His friends absolutely loved what I made for him and started begging me to make stuff for them and their fraternity!

From there, GottCustoms was born!

I have since made everything ranging from t-shirts, hoodies, and sweatpants to posters and flags to trucker hats and graduation caps.

Again, the name "GottCustoms" is extremely near and dear to my heart. Like "GottPhotos," I was inspired by my nickname and love for dairy products. I am beyond ecstatic and grateful to continue to use this name on my store today.

I even designed my logo with my aforementioned inspiration in mind. It is indeed a milk carton combined with a clothes hanger; the perfect representation of "gotmilk?" and a symbol of my custom merchandise!

My main sales channel is Etsy. But I also have a Shopify and an Instagram dedicated to my brand.

Finally, because I am passionate about the environment, I ensure that my merchandise and distribution methods are eco-friendly and sustainable.
