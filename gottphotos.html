GottPhotos

The name "GottPhotos" is very special to me. "Gott" is part of my last name and it has always been my nickname from friends and teammates.

Growing up, I had always been obsessed with photography and videography. When I created my very first photography-based social media account to highlight my work, I drew inspiration from the "gotmilk?" posters in my school cafeteria and knew my page had to be under the name "GottPhotos."

Since then, the name has stuck with me in every one of my endeavors, including my custom merchandise brand, GottCustoms.
